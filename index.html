Hi, I'm Krista and I'm passionate about building human centered products. Yes, I code, and I design.

I'm lead mobile app product designer at DICK'S Sporting Goods, and Android developer. Being a designer developer means it's my strength to deeply care about the human experience and the "why", and then translate that into the best "how" to meet and surpass the user's expectation through design and code. I also freelance consult with startups such as SignEasy and PregBuddy on everything UX. I am a proud alum from Penn State's College of IST: Product Design and Development as their December 2015 class student marshal.
